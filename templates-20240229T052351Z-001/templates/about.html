Welcome to the Future of Garlic Production!

At Mariano Marcos State University, we are pioneers in the development of IoT-based and controlled environment nurseries tailored specifically for G0 garlic production. With a passion for innovation and sustainable agriculture, we have harnessed the power of modern technology to revolutionize the way garlic is grown.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to empower garlic farmers and enthusiasts with cutting-edge solutions that not only increase yield but also ensure the highest quality garlic production. We aim to make G0 garlic cultivation more efficient, reliable, and environmentally friendly.

Who We Are:

We are a group of student researchers with a deep commitment to pushing the boundaries of traditional farming methods. Our journey began as a quest for innovative and sustainable solutions in agriculture, and we've merged our academic backgrounds with hands-on experience to bring you a unique approach to garlic farming.

Our Focus:

Innovation: Our student perspective ensures fresh, cutting-edge ideas to enhance garlic farming.

Quality: We aim for nothing less than the highest quality garlic bulbs.

Simplicity: Our solutions are designed to make garlic farming accessible and straightforward for all.

Why Us:

Student Vision: We offer a fresh take on garlic farming, driven by the curiosity and dedication of student researchers.

Guidance: Count on us for friendly and accessible support to help you succeed in your garlic farming endeavors.

Efficiency: We streamline the farming process, allowing you to make the most of your resources.

Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable farming practices to reduce your ecological footprint.

Quality Output: Our technology promises exceptional garlic and improved yield.
